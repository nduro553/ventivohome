Our Padel Training Programs

Beginner Fundamentals

Start your padel adventure with our comprehensive beginner program. Learn proper grip techniques, basic positioning, and foundational strokes. Our certified instructors ensure you build a strong foundation for future advancement. Classes are structured to help you understand court dynamics and develop confidence in every shot.

Duration: 6-8 weeks | Frequency: 2-3 sessions per week

Intermediate Development

Progress to the next level with advanced technique refinement and tactical awareness. This program focuses on improving your consistency, developing strategic play, and enhancing your competitive edge. You'll learn advanced positioning, net play strategies, and match tactics that separate intermediate players from beginners.

Duration: 8-10 weeks | Frequency: 3 sessions per week

Advanced Competition

Reach your peak performance with our advanced competition program. Designed for serious players aiming for tournament success, this intensive training covers advanced tactics, mental conditioning, and match preparation. Our coaches provide personalized feedback and competitive match simulations to prepare you for real tournament scenarios.

Duration: 10-12 weeks | Frequency: 4+ sessions per week

Our Journey Begins

Ventivo Home was founded in 2015 with a singular vision: to democratize padel tennis training and make it accessible to everyone. Our founders, passionate padel enthusiasts themselves, recognized a gap in the market for quality coaching and community-driven training programs. What started as a small operation with two courts has grown into a thriving padel academy serving hundreds of players annually.

Our commitment to excellence has remained unwavering throughout our growth. We invested heavily in facility improvements, coach training, and player development programs. Today, Ventivo Home stands as a beacon of quality padel instruction in the region, known for producing competitive players and fostering a welcoming community atmosphere.

Coaching Philosophy & Methodology

At Ventivo Home, we believe in a holistic approach to padel development. Our coaching philosophy emphasizes technical excellence, tactical awareness, and mental resilience. We employ evidence-based training methods combined with personalized feedback to accelerate player development. Each coach undergoes continuous professional development to stay current with the latest padel coaching techniques and sports science research. Our methodology focuses on building confidence, fostering a love for the game, and developing players who can compete at their highest potential. Expansion & Excellence

By 2018, Ventivo Home expanded to include six professional courts and a dedicated coaching staff of twelve certified instructors. Our facility became known for hosting regional tournaments and training elite players. We established partnerships with national padel associations and began developing young talent through our youth academy program.

The expansion phase brought new challenges and opportunities. We invested in state-of-the-art video analysis technology, hired sports psychologists, and developed specialized training programs for competitive players. Our commitment to innovation and player development attracted attention from padel federations and media outlets, establishing Ventivo Home as a leader in padel coaching and development.
